# Interview Prompt — Build Audience

When the creator invokes `/build-audience` and either (a) creator_identity_matrix is below 30% or (b) the creator has insufficient real-world VOC sources, run interview-mode in this order. Cluster by section so the creator can answer 30 minutes at a time.

## Cluster 1 — Sources of real-world VOC
1. Do you have sales call recordings or transcripts? How many?
2. Do you have a comment archive from a prior channel or social account? Can you export it?
3. Do you have DMs or emails where the audience describes their pain? How many?
4. Have you ever surveyed your audience? What questions and how many responses?
5. Have you done customer onboarding interviews or post-program interviews? How many?

If 4+ sources are absent, pause and recommend: spend 2 weeks gathering 3+ sources before continuing. Real-world signal beats every interview.

## Cluster 2 — Demographics
6. What is the audience's age range, narrowed to 5–8 years?
7. Gender split %?
8. Geographic distribution?
9. Income range — actual numbers, not tiers?
10. Education and role/industry?

## Cluster 3 — Life-stage / firmographic
11. What stage of life or business are they in?
12. What major events in the last 12 months are likely to have happened to them?
13. Who do they live with / who's affected by their decisions?

## Cluster 4 — Psychographic (4-layer pain)
14. What pain do they say publicly?
15. What pain do they feel that they don't quite say?
16. What pain do they have privately (would tell a therapist or close friend, not a stranger)?
17. What pain might they not even admit to themselves?

## Cluster 5 — Psychographic (4-layer desire)
18. What outcome do they want functionally?
19. How do they want to feel?
20. Who do they want to become?
21. What do they want to leave behind?

## Cluster 6 — Beliefs
22. List 8 things this audience currently believes — at least 4 of which the channel will challenge or reframe.
23. What do they want to believe about themselves post-transformation?
24. Of the limiting belief triad (Worthless / Helpless / Hopeless), which is dominant — and what 3 behaviors prove it?

## Cluster 7 — Voice of Viewer (the verbatim mining)
25. Pull 20+ verbatim phrases from your VOC sources. Tag each with source.
26. Pull 15+ verbatim pain phrases.
27. Pull 15+ verbatim desire phrases.
28. Pull 15+ verbatim objection phrases.
29. What in-group phrase does this audience use that outsiders don't recognize?

## Cluster 8 — Awareness levels
30. Estimate the % of your target market at each of the 5 awareness levels.
31. Which level is your channel primarily speaking to?
32. What 3 specific behaviors prove that level (search terms, comment themes, click patterns)?

## Cluster 9 — Sophistication
33. Sophistication stage: Naive / Aware / Skeptical / Exhausted? Why?
34. How many similar offers has this audience already been pitched?

## Cluster 10 — Behavioral
35. What 3–5 channel archetypes does this audience watch besides yours?
36. When and how do they consume content?
37. Name 5–10 specific external shock events that make them buy.
38. Where do they search for help?
39. What 5+ trust signals do they require before paying?

## Cluster 11 — Objections
40. List 10+ objections this audience has, in their own words. For each: what's the underlying belief? What reframes it? What proof do they need?

## Synthesis
After clusters, run the document per SKILL.md Output Format. Score each section's confidence. Run the 3am Test on Section 4. Run the hook-readiness test (can you produce 10 hooks from this doc immediately? if no, deepen specifics). Return the document with explicit gap flags.
