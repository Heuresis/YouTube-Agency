# Interview Prompt — Extract Creator Voice

When source artifacts are insufficient, run interview-mode.

## Cluster 1 — Source artifacts (priority)
1. Send 5+ long-form pieces you wrote (blog posts, newsletters, scripts).
2. Send 3+ sales call recordings or transcripts.
3. Send 10+ personal social posts (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, etc.).
4. Send 10+ unscripted minutes of speaking (podcast, interview, voice memo).
5. Send your About page + channel-trailer script if existing.

## Cluster 2 — Communication style
6. How would you describe your communication style — direct / consultative / storytelling / analytical / contrarian?
7. Pick 3 long passages from your work that exemplify your voice. Why these?

## Cluster 3 — Tone
8. Primary tone (one word)?
9. Secondary tone (modifier)?
10. What's a piece you wrote where the tone felt exactly right?

## Cluster 4 — Personality traits
11. List 3-5 personality traits (one word each + 1 supporting passage from your work).

## Cluster 5 — Cadence patterns
12. What sentence cadence patterns appear repeatedly in your writing? (E.g., "X. But here's the thing — Y.")
13. Pull 5 verbatim instances of each pattern.

## Cluster 6 — Phrases
14. List 20+ phrases you use repeatedly (verbatim from your work).
15. List 10-30 phrases you actively avoid (your own anti-phrases beyond the canonical banned list).

## Cluster 7 — Persuasion style
16. How do you persuade — authority-by-proof / story-first / mechanism-first / contrarian-provocation?
17. What's the structural arc of your typical persuasion piece?

## Cluster 8 — Authority positioning
18. Are you a category-king / challenger / insider / outsider-with-edge?
19. What earns your authority?

## Cluster 9 — On-camera persona
20. How do you deliver — high-energy / measured / contrarian / warm-instructional / confessional?
21. Posture default? Framing default? Pace (wpm)?
22. Pause rhythm — rare / measured / frequent?
23. Energy arc — flat / rising / wave / front-loaded?
24. Signature visual elements?

## Cluster 10 — Parasocial-trust contract
25. What 5+ things will you always do for the audience long-term?
26. What 5+ things will you never do, even if profitable?
27. How does your on-camera persona relate to the off-camera person?

## Cluster 11 — Positioning
28. Named mechanism (2-4 words)?
29. Category position?
30. 5+ contrarian beliefs in your niche?

## Cluster 12 — Archetype
31. Primary archetype: long-form-VSL specialist / contrarian-narrator / analytical-explainer / story-driven creator / contrast-format viral specialist?
32. Secondary archetype?

## Synthesis
Run the document. Schedule a Blind Output Test sample (3 voice-faithful sample outputs vs. 3 creator-written samples; evaluate when ready).
