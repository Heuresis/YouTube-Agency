- Mode: n/a

## Input
"My audience is The Trapped Operator (Helpless / Capability transformation). I want to design my $8K group coaching program. They have 6 prior failed delegation attempts on average. They need a Capability transformation."

```markdown
# Offer Document — The Step-Out Program

**Offer Type:** Group coaching cohort (12 weeks, 8–12 operators per cohort)
**Price Point:** $8,000 one-time (or 3 × $2,975)
**Target Avatar:** The Trapped Operator
**Transformation Type:** Capability
**Date:** 2026-05-03
**Confidence:** HIGH

## 1. Core Promise
"From doing 80% of delivery to running the business 80% of the time, in 12 weeks, using the Role-Design-First framework + 4 weekly accompaniment calls + a delegation-reset playbook."

## 2. Unique Mechanism
**Name:** Role-Design-First framework
**What it is:** A 4-step methodology that designs the work into delegate-able roles BEFORE hiring or systemizing. Most delegation fails because the founder hires for "things I do" instead of "the role this work belongs to." Role-Design-First reverses the sequence: define the role's outputs and decision-rights first, then hire / promote / contract for the role.
**What it differentiates against:** Generic delegation content that focuses on "find the right person" or "build SOPs." Role-Design-First says: SOPs come third, hiring comes second, role design comes first.
**Key insights captured:**
- Most operators delegate tasks, not roles — that's why delegation fails repeatedly
- Roles need decision-rights, not just task-lists, to operate without the founder
- The founder's discomfort with delegation is structural (poor role design), not characterological (need more confidence)

## 3. Transformation Delivered
- Before: founder personally produces 70–80% of delivery output, oversees 100% of decisions, can't take 5 days off without intervention
- After: founder produces ≤ 20% of delivery output, oversees only escalation-level decisions, can take 4-week absence without delivery quality drop
- Timeframe: 12 weeks for the structural transition; 6–9 months to full habituation
- Required commitment: 5–7 hours/week of program work + willingness to redesign 2–3 roles in their business

## 6. Qualification
### Must-have criteria
- Self-employed service provider (consulting, agency, professional services)
- Annual revenue $150K+ (otherwise the math on a 12-week program timing doesn't work)
- 6+ months in business with at least one delivery process they currently personally do
- Willingness to commit 5+ hours/week to program work for 12 weeks

### Must-not-have criteria
- Pre-revenue / early-stage operators (this isn't a launch program)
- Looking for hiring help only without role-design openness
- Solo operators who don't intend to ever delegate (program is wrong fit)
- Looking for a VA-management course (different problem)

### Filtering mechanism
Application form → 25-min discovery call → enrollment

## 7. Guarantee + Risk Reversal
- Guarantee structure: **Skill-acquisition guarantee** — if the participant completes all 12 weeks of the program, attends 80%+ of calls, and submits all 4 role-design exercises, and still does not have a delegate-able role designed by week 12, the program refunds in full + the participant keeps all materials.
- Audience fear it absorbs: Helplessness ("I can't make delegation work") — the guarantee says "if you do the work, the framework produces a result. If it doesn't, it's our problem, not yours."

## 8. Offer Ladder
- Lead magnet: "The 6 Failure Patterns of Service-Business Delegation" (PDF) — diagnostic that surfaces which pattern the operator falls into
- Tripwire: optional $97 self-paced "Role-Design-First mini-course" — 90-minute video + workbook
- **Core offer: The Step-Out Program ($8,000)** — this document
- Upsell: "Hire-and-Onboard" 30-day add-on ($2,500) — for graduates who are now hiring into the designed role
- High-ticket: "Founder-to-Owner" mastermind ($25,000/year, 12-month program) — for graduates ready to move from operator to owner with multiple roles delegated
- Continuity: Alumni community ($97/month after first 12 weeks)

## 9. Economics Validation
- LTV per customer:
  - $8,000 core
  - 30% attach rate on $2,500 upsell = $750
  - 15% attach rate on $25K mastermind 6 months later = $3,750
  - 40% attach rate on $97/mo continuity for avg 14 months = $543
  - **LTV total: $13,043**
- CAC estimate:
  - Channel production cost: $40K/year amortized over 60 enrollments = $667
  - Sales operations (call setter + closer fees): $400 per enrolled
  - Ad spend if any: $0 organic / up to $300 if paid retargeting added
  - **CAC total: $1,067 (organic) — $1,367 (with retargeting)**
- LTV:CAC ratio: $13,043 / $1,067 = **12.2× organic / 9.5× with retargeting** — **PASS** (well above 3:1 floor)
- Gross margin: 75% (program delivery costs are coach time + community platform + materials)
- Breakeven cohort: 12 customers (covers fixed costs of cohort operations + amortizes annual production)
- Channel-stage fit: appropriate for 5K+ subscriber channels with engaged email list ≥ 500
```
