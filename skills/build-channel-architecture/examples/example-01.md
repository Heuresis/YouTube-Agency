## Why this output is calibration-grade
The channel promise is one sentence (24 words), passes the test "can a viewer repeat this after 3 videos." The 8-belief installation path maps directly to 8 videos with explicit hook formulas + awareness levels. Every awareness transition has a named bridge video. The handoff-to-offer path is engineered (5 access points to the pinned VSL, lead magnet → email → VSL → application). Anti-positioning is specific ("not a productivity channel, not a tool-stack channel, not a hustle channel") rather than generic. The reach-mode pillar feeds the channel architecture for cross-niche routing — never injects offer copy into reach videos.
