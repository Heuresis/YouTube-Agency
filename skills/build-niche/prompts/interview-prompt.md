# Interview Prompt — Build Niche

When the creator invokes `/build-niche` and the inputs are insufficient (no offer defined, no domain background, no candidate niches), enter interview mode. Ask in this order — one cluster at a time.

## Cluster 1 — Creator depth
1. What domain have you spent the most hours in over the last 5 years? (work, study, lived experience)
2. If you made a video on this topic every week for 5 years (250+ videos), would you become a better version of yourself, or worse?
3. What part of this domain do you have an opinion on that most people in the niche don't?

## Cluster 2 — Offer reality
4. Do you have a paid offer right now? If yes: name, price, transformation, current customer count.
5. If no offer yet: what offer do you intend to build? Group program? 1:1? Course? What price tier?
6. Who is the offer for, in 1 sentence? (Demographic + outcome they want)

## Cluster 3 — Audience-offer fit
7. Does the audience you'd naturally attract on YouTube have the budget for this offer? Be honest.
8. If the audience can't pay the offer's price, are you willing to (a) reframe the offer down, (b) reframe the audience up, or (c) accept a long ramp to monetization?

## Cluster 4 — Differentiation
9. Of the 6 Differentiation Paths, which can you credibly own?
   - Unique mechanism (a process / system you've built)
   - Unique enemy (a belief or practice you publicly oppose)
   - Unique audience segment (a sub-segment underserved by current creators)
   - Unique format (a structural pattern competitors don't use)
   - Unique credibility source (lived experience, credentials, insider access)
   - Unique philosophy (a worldview that orients the channel)
10. Of the channels currently dominant in your niche (described as archetypes, not names): what do they do well? What do they do poorly?

## Cluster 5 — Anti-positioning
11. What 5–10 topics in your niche would you refuse to make a video on, even if it would perform?
12. What are you NOT? (Other creators in this niche tend to be X — you are explicitly not X because Y)

## Cluster 6 — Reach-mode pool
13. Beyond your primary niche, what 3–5 adjacent topics share the same dominant limiting belief? (Same audience pain, different surface topic.)
14. For each adjacent niche: would a viewer who watches this content also be a candidate for your offer?

## Synthesis
After the cluster answers, run the 3-vector scoring + 6 Differentiation Paths test + 5-year longevity test per the SKILL.md process. Return the niche definition document.

If any cluster has < 60% answers populated, flag the gap and recommend the creator spend 30 minutes filling before proceeding to `/build-audience`.
