# INBOX — raw idea capture (team, comments, DMs, calls)

> The catch-all for **raw, untriaged video ideas** — from the team, from comments, from sales calls, from the creator's voice memos. Capture is **faithful**: titles, thumbnail directions, summaries, references, partial scripts — verbatim, even when the idea is half-formed or off-strategy. Triage happens *after* capture, never instead of it. A killed idea stays on the page with its kill reason — the record of what you moved away from is strategy data.
>
> **This file is not the plan.** The ranked, proof-gated plan is [`SLATE.md`](SLATE.md). Nothing films from the INBOX directly.

---

## Triage statuses

Every idea block carries exactly one status at the top:

| Status | Meaning | What happens next |
|---|---|---|
| `NEW` | Captured, not yet triaged. | Triage at the weekly slate review. |
| `PROMOTED → SLATE #n` | Survived triage; a proof row backs it. | Scored row added to `SLATE.md`; brief follows. |
| `RECAST` | The instinct is right, the framing is off-strategy. | Note the recast direction; the recast version gets its own SLATE row. |
| `PARKED` | Good idea, wrong quarter (no proof row yet / off the current funnel push). | Revisit when the gather or the calendar catches up. |
| `KILLED` | Off-strategy. | Keep the block + write the kill reason. Never delete. |

**Triage rules (run each `NEW` idea through, in order):**
1. **Kill-list check** (`workspace/STRATEGY.md`) — trend-jacking the channel can't honestly own, commodity hype formats, anything the strategy explicitly killed. Fails → `KILLED` + reason.
2. **Proof check** (`workspace/PROOF-BANK.md`) — is there a row that backs the core claim? Yes → eligible for `PROMOTED`. No, but gatherable → `PARKED` with the gather item named. No, and not gatherable → `KILLED` or `RECAST`.
3. **Conversion-assist check** — would the target buyer show up warmer after watching (the SLATE scoring rubric)? Pure-reach ideas can pass, but only as the strategy's declared reach share (INV-12 mode mix).
4. **Engagement-bait check** (INV-17) + **banned-vocab scan** (INV-7) on any drafted titles/hooks — strip violations during promotion, don't carry them into the SLATE.
