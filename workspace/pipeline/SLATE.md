# SLATE — the ranked next-videos board

> The single ranked list of what gets made next. Built with the `/idea-farm` methodology, sourced from **`workspace/PROOF-BANK.md` — real builds with real numbers** — and from the reads in `workspace/published/_ANALYTICS.md`. These are videos no other channel can make, because no other channel has your proof rows. That's the moat.
>
> **Scoring rule: conversion-assist, not raw views.** The score answers one question — *does a {{offer-price}} buyer who watches this show up warmer?* — reach is the tie-breaker, never the ranker. **Gating rule (INV-6):** a video whose core number is not `[VERIFIED]` stays **`gated`** no matter its score; the moment the proof row verifies, it moves to **`film this week`** and a dated script lands in `workspace/film-this-week/`.

**How to score (conversion-assist /10):**
- **+0–4 · proof weight** — is the core number `[VERIFIED]` (4) / `[CONFIRM]` (2) / `[NEEDS-PROOF]` (0)? A proof channel's score is mostly its receipt.
- **+0–3 · buyer-question fit** — does it squarely answer one of `workspace/STRATEGY.md`'s buyer questions ("can they do this?" / "for someone like me?" / "is this real?")?
- **+0–2 · funnel position** — does it feed the one CTA destination this quarter is driving?
- **+0–1 · reach upside** — honest tie-breaker only. A 9-reach/2-conversion idea loses to a 4-reach/8-conversion idea here, every time.

## The real bottleneck (this, not ideas)

A proof-gated slate isn't idea-starved — it's **proof-gated.** Every case study can be designed and voice-ready while it waits; what it needs is the **PROOF-BANK gather** to put real numbers on screen (a fuzzy number kills a proof channel — INV-5/6). Keep the gather list explicit:

1. {{N real invoices, names blocked, line + date visible}} — unlocks {{#s}}.
2. {{One real client case study, fully: before-number, build, after-number, a dashboard to screen-record}} — ships the series opener.
3. {{The boards/diagrams from actual builds}} — the centerpiece B-roll.
4. Confirm: {{each [CONFIRM] figure currently blocking a scripted video}}.

> Close the gather and the gated rows flip to **film this week**. That's the move — not more ideas.
