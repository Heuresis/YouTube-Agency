# Outro Specimen 003 — Nurture / lead-magnet outro

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Beat 1 (0–20s): Recap the value delivered. "By now, you have [specific takeaway 1], [specific takeaway 2], and [specific takeaway 3]."
- Beat 2 (20–60s): Tease deeper resources. "If you want to go deeper on this, I've put together [specific lead magnet — guide, template, course]. It's free."
- Beat 3 (60–90s): Specific lead-magnet pitch. "It includes [specific component 1], [specific component 2], and [specific component 3]. Click the [specific link location] to get it."
- Beat 4 (90–120s): Sign-off with continuity. "Thanks for watching. Make sure you're subscribed because next week I'm going to break down [specific upcoming topic]."

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The nurture-outro extracts an email / phone number / channel-follow at the end of high-value videos. Beat 3's specific component breakdown converts the lead magnet from a vague "free guide" into a specific artifact the audience perceives as valuable.

## When to use
Authority-mode videos where the operator has a meaningful lead-magnet sequence built. Less effective without genuine downstream nurture infrastructure.
