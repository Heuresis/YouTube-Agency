# Outro Specimen 015 — Fast-recap end-card outro

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Beat 1 (0–15s): Two-sentence recap. "So here's the takeaway: [single line]. Apply it this week."
- Beat 2 (15–30s): Single explicit ask, low-friction. "If this helped, drop a comment with [specific question] — I read every one."
- Beat 3 (30–50s): Next-video hand-off. "If you want the next layer of this, [next video] is the natural follow-up. It's right here on screen."
- Beat 4 (50–60s): On-screen end card with two video thumbnails.
- Beat 5 (60–65s): Sign-off. "See you in the next one."

## Why this works (the imitation lesson — 2–4 sentences)
Fast-recap end-card outros work for high-volume channels where the goal is binge-watching rather than conversion. The single-line takeaway (Beat 1) maximizes recall in a viewer who's about to autoplay the next video. The "drop a comment with X" ask (Beat 2) is the load-bearing engagement mechanism — comments boost algorithmic ranking, and asking a specific question outperforms generic asks.
