# Script Section Specimen 011 — Three-case-study social-proof stack body

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Beat 1 (10–30s): Detailed case study #1. "[Student type] came to me [N months ago] with [specific vulnerable starting state]. Today, [specific impressive outcome]." [Show on-camera artifact / testimonial.]
- Beat 2 (30–60s): Detailed case study #2 — different demographic. "[Different student type], [different starting state]. Same outcome: [specific impressive result]." [Show artifact.]
- Beat 3 (60–90s): Detailed case study #3 — counter-stereotype demographic. "Even [counter-stereotype demographic — typically older, less tech-savvy, more skeptical] [specific result]." [Show artifact.]
- Beat 4 (90–120s): Aggregate quantified summary. "Across [N students] over [N timeframe], the average result is [specific aggregate metric]."

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The social-proof-stacked section uses the Social Proof Hierarchy. Three detailed case studies (rank 1) consistently outperform a hundred generic testimonials (rank 7). The structural rule: each case study must have specific starting state, specific outcome, and specific timeframe — generic testimonials get filtered out as fake.

## When to use
Authority-mode videos at the conversion phase (typically 60–80% through the video). Pairs naturally with the 15-step VSL spine where this section is "Testimonials & Receipts" (Step 8).
