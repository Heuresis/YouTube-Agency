# Thumbnail Specimen 008 — Contrast emoji / icon stack

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Visual: Two contrasting emoji / icon elements (e.g., a happy face vs a sad face, a money icon vs a broken icon, a check mark vs an X).
- Text overlay: 2–4 words anchoring the contrast.

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The contrast emoji thumbnail signals at a glance that the video is about the difference between two states. Especially effective for audiences who scroll quickly — the contrast is read in <1 second.

## When to use
Reach-mode content where the contrast is the central narrative. Less effective for nuanced or multi-element narratives.
