# Thumbnail Specimen 023 — Extreme-emotion-face thumbnail

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Visual: Operator's face zoomed in tightly — fills 70%+ of the thumbnail. Strong emotional expression — shock, disbelief, fear, awe. Eyes wide.
- Text overlay: 1–3 words. Bold sans-serif, often white with thick outline or accent color.
- Color: 2–3 colors — face's natural tones + strong accent for text.

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Extreme-emotion-face thumbnails work because the face dominates the visual field and triggers the brain's automatic social-evaluation response within milliseconds. The expression is the load-bearing element; mild or neutral expressions don't trigger the same response as visibly intense ones. Single-word text amplifies the emotional read; full sentences distract from the face.

## When to use
- mode: reach
- audience awareness: unaware
- pairs with: don't-do-until title or self-experiment title, curiosity-gap hook
- channel stage: 10K–100K, 100K–1M
