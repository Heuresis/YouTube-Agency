# Thumbnail Specimen 018 — Creator-business analytics thumbnail

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Visual: Operator left, analytics graph or revenue-dashboard right (blurred for authenticity, but the trend visible).
- Text overlay: A revenue-per-subscriber stat or counterintuitive ratio ("$50K from 8K SUBS"). Bold.
- Color: 2–3 colors max, often with green for revenue / growth visual.

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Creator-business analytics thumbnails work because the audience is intensely numerically literate about creator-success patterns. The counterintuitive ratio (high revenue from small audience) is the load-bearing element — generic "I make money" visuals don't trigger evaluation. The blurred analytics signal real-but-protected, which is the right authenticity tier.

## When to use
- mode: authority
- audience awareness: solution-aware
- pairs with: small-audience-big-revenue title, PAST-formula hook
- channel stage: 100K–1M
