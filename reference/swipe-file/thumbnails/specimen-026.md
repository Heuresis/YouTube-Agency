# Thumbnail Specimen 026 — Opinion-quote thumbnail

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Visual: Operator's face with confident / thoughtful expression. Often facing the camera directly with a quote-mark or speech-bubble visual.
- Text overlay: A short opinion statement in quote format. 4–8 words. ("[Common practice] doesn't work" or "I'd never [common action]").
- Color: 2 colors — neutral face + bold accent for quote text.

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Opinion-quote thumbnails work because they pre-load the click with a verbal commitment from the creator — the audience clicks to see the defense of the opinion. The quote-mark visual is load-bearing; it converts the text from generic title-overlay to a "real thing the creator said." The confident expression authenticates the opinion as considered rather than reactive.

## When to use
- mode: authority
- audience awareness: solution-aware
- pairs with: stop-and-do title or common-belief-is-wrong title, contrarian-formula hook
- channel stage: 100K–1M
