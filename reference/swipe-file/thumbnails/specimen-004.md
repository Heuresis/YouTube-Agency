# Thumbnail Specimen 004 — Comparison split-frame (X vs Y)

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Visual: Split-frame thumbnail. Left side shows logo / image / icon for option A. Right side shows logo / image / icon for option B. Optional: a "VS" element in the middle.
- Text overlay: 2–4 words anchoring the comparison ("X vs Y," "WHICH PAYS MORE?", "WHO WINS?").

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The comparison thumbnail signals to the audience that the video will resolve the X-vs-Y question they're already debating. Most effective when the comparison is genuinely contested in the audience's mind.

## When to use
Comparison-format videos. Less effective when the comparison is one-sided.
