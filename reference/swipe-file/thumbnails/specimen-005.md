# Thumbnail Specimen 005 — Founder + product

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Visual: The operator visible in the frame, holding / interacting with / pointing at the product / artifact / output of their work. Background contextual to the product.
- Text overlay: Brief title fragment supporting the visual narrative.

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The founder-plus-product thumbnail reinforces the personal-brand layer. The operator's presence connects the artifact to a specific person (not anonymous content); the artifact gives the operator something tangible to demonstrate authority over.

## When to use
When the operator's brand is built on personal credibility and the product / artifact is photogenic. Less effective when the artifact is digital / abstract / unphotographable.
