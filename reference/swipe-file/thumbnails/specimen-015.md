# Thumbnail Specimen 015 — Same-pose before-after thumbnail

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Visual: Two photos of the operator side-by-side. Same pose, same lighting, same camera angle. Only the body composition / physical state differs.
- Text overlay: A specific timeframe or metric ("90 DAYS" or "-30 LBS"). Bold, sans-serif.
- Color: Neutral / consistent across both halves to make the body the only variable.

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Same-pose before-after thumbnails are higher-trust than typical before-after splits because the controlled variables (pose, lighting, angle) signal scientific honesty rather than flattering staging. The visible discipline of matching the photos becomes itself a credibility signal. The viewer auto-calculates "could I make this transition?" within seconds.
