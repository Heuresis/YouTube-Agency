# Thumbnail Specimen 022 — Cinematic-documentary thumbnail

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Visual: Single high-production photograph — landscape, portrait, or detail shot. Often dark or moody color grading. No emoji-style elements.
- Text overlay: 3–6 words in clean serif or modern sans-serif, often centered.
- Color: Cinematic color palette — deep blues, warm sepias, dramatic contrasts.

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Cinematic-documentary thumbnails work because they signal premium production tier — the audience self-selects to invest more attention before clicking. The dark / moody color grading is a counter-position to the maximalist YouTube norm, which makes the thumbnail stand out in feeds dominated by bright reds and yellows. The serif typography signals editorial / journalistic content.

- mode: reach
- audience awareness: unaware

- channel stage: 100K–1M, 1M+
