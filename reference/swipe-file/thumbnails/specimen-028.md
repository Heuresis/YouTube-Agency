# Thumbnail Specimen 028 — Lifestyle-aspiration thumbnail

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Visual: Aspirational lifestyle imagery — operator in a setting that signals the desired identity (workspace, environment, attire). Often soft natural light.
- Text overlay: Identity-statement framing — "BECOME [IDENTITY]" or "[IDENTITY] WHO [OUTCOME]". 4–6 words.
- Color: Warm, natural palette + 1 accent for text.

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Lifestyle-aspiration thumbnails work because they trigger identity-projection — the audience imagines themselves as the version of self the visual represents. The lifestyle imagery is load-bearing; the aspiration must look attainable but specific. Generic luxury imagery underperforms specific lifestyle visuals (e.g., a particular kind of workspace) that the target audience self-identifies with.
