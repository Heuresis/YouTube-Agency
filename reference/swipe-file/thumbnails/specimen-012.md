# Thumbnail Specimen 012 — Founder-with-receipt thumbnail

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Visual: Operator (often standing, arms crossed or pointing) on left two-thirds. On right, a blurred but clearly recognizable dashboard / P&L / revenue screenshot.
- Text overlay: 2–3 words featuring a specific dollar amount or percentage (e.g., "$127K MONTH" or "+340%").
- Color: 2 colors max — operator's neutral background vs accent (often green for revenue, red for negative-then-positive).

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Founder-with-receipt thumbnails leverage social-proof at the visual layer — the dashboard isn't readable but is unmistakably "real." The blur is intentional; sharp dashboards trigger fact-checking impulses, blurred ones signal authenticity. The specific dollar amount on the text overlay is load-bearing — generic "MASSIVE GROWTH" doesn't work where "$127K" does.
