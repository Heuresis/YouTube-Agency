# Title Specimen 037 — Complete-guide title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"The Complete Guide to [Topic] (Everything You Need)"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "The Complete Beginner's Guide to [Domain]"
- "Everything You Need to Know About [Topic] in [N Minutes]"
- "[Topic]: The Full Walkthrough (No Filler)"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Complete-guide titles work because they offer the audience cognitive closure — one video instead of a research rabbit hole. The "no filler" variant is high-leverage because it pre-empts the universal complaint about long-form content. The "N minutes" anchor adds value-density signaling.

## When this specimen is in the candidate pool
- mode: authority
- audience awareness: solution-aware

- channel stage: 100K–1M
