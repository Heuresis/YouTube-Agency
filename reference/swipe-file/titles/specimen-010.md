# Title Specimen 010 — "DON'T do X" reactive title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"DON'T [Common-Practice Activity], Do This Instead"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "DON'T [Start Niche-X Affiliate Marketing], Do This Instead"
- "DON'T [Start Running Ads], Watch This First"
- "DON'T [Niche Down on Your Channel], Do This Instead"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The "DON'T" title is the most direct form of pattern interrupt. The "Do This Instead" framing must promise a specific alternative — without it, the title reads as generic "don't do that" doomerism.

## When to use
Reactive-content slot, especially when responding to a trending advice or platform change. Loses effectiveness rapidly when over-used.
