# Title Specimen 041 — Identity-pivot title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"Become the Kind of [Identity / Person] Who [Outcome]"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "How to Become the Kind of [Operator / Creator / Lifter] Who [Specific Outcome]"
- "The [Identity] You Need to Become to [Outcome]"
- "Stop Trying to [Outcome] — Become the Person Who Does"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Identity-pivot titles work because they reframe the goal from doing to being — the audience knows tactics aren't the bottleneck, identity is. "Stop trying — become" is the load-bearing inversion that makes the title feel philosophically loaded. This pattern self-selects for audiences past the tactical-tourist stage.
