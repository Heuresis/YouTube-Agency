# Title Specimen 017 — Stat-inversion title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"[Specific number / percent] of [audience] are [counterintuitive state]"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "[N%] of [Income Bracket] Earners Will Never [Outcome]"
- "Only [N%] of [Audience] Actually [Achieve Outcome] — Here's Why"
- "The [Numerical Claim] About [Domain] No One Wants to Admit"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Stat-inversion titles work because they trigger immediate curiosity — the viewer wants to know if they're in the [N%] that's failing. The specific number is load-bearing; "most people" doesn't trigger the same response as "73% of people." The "no one wants to admit" framing converts the title from observation to insider claim.
