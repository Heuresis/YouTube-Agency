# Title Specimen 002 — Bound-promise formula

## Structure (paraphrased)
"How I [Achieved Result] in [Timeframe] ([Qualifier])"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "How I [Hit $10K Months] in [90 Days] ([Starting From Zero])"
- "How I [Built a 7-Figure Brand] in [12 Months] ([With No Capital])"
- "How I [Closed My First Client] in [30 Days] ([Without Cold Calls])"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The time-bound formula activates the comparative-self mechanism. The qualifier ("starting from zero," "without cold calls") is the most load-bearing element — it pre-empts the disqualifying objection.

## When to use
Mid-tier title slot. Especially effective when the operator can credibly anchor the timeframe to a specific real event.
