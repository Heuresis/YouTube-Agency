# Title Specimen 001 — Curiosity-gap formula

## Structure (paraphrased)
"The [Specific Thing] Nobody Talks About That [Specific Result]"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "The [Marketing Lever] Nobody Talks About That [3x'd My Reply Rate]"
- "The [YouTube Metric] Nobody Talks About That [Got My Channel to 100K Subs]"
- "The [Sales Mistake] Nobody Talks About That [Cost Me $50K]"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The curiosity-gap formula activates the brain's information-asymmetry response. Specificity in [Specific Thing] is critical — generic "the trick" or "the hack" doesn't trigger the response; specific "the marketing lever" or "the YouTube metric" does. The result must be quantified.

## When to use
High-CTR title slot. Especially effective in saturated niches where audiences are desensitized to generic advice.
