# Title Specimen 006 — Contrast / identity-pivot title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"I Left [High-Status Conventional Path] To Start A [Specific Revenue] Business So You Can Copy Me"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "I Left [High-Status Career] To Start A [$6M/Yr] Business So You Can Copy Me"
- "I Quit [Top-Tier Firm] To Build A [$1M ARR Online Brand] In 18 Months"
- "I Walked Away From [Established Career Path] To Make My First [$100K Online]"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The contrast / identity-pivot title puts the audience in the operator's shoes via "So You Can Copy Me." The "high-status conventional path" being downgraded is the social-proof signal — without a recognizable high-status credential, the title reads as just another business claim.

## When to use
When the operator has a specific identity-pivot story (job-quit, school-quit, geography-shift). Less effective without a specific pivot.
