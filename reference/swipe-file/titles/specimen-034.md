# Title Specimen 034 — Tech-mystery title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"Why [Tech / Phenomenon] Is [Non-Obvious Characterization]"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "Why [Common Technology] Is Actually [Counterintuitive Fact]"
- "How [Tech] Really Works (And Why It Matters)"
- "The Hidden Architecture Behind [Common Product / Tool]"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Tech-mystery titles work because they tap into the brain's response to "I use this every day but never thought about how it works." The non-obvious characterization is load-bearing; "[tech] is broken" or "[tech] is actually X" creates curiosity-gap density that "[tech] explained" doesn't. The "and why it matters" close converts curiosity into stake.
