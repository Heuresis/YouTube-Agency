# Title Specimen 016 — WARNING title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"WARNING: This [Practice] Might Make Your [Outcome] An Extra [Specific Result] Next [Timeframe]"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "WARNING: This [Funnel Tweak] Might Make Your [Info Product] An Extra [$100K] Next Month"
- "WARNING: [Common Practice] Is Costing You [$X/Year] in Lost Revenue"
- "WARNING: [Specific Mistake] Is Killing Your [Conversion Rate]"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The "WARNING" framing creates urgency through anticipated loss. The all-caps element is critical — softer phrasings ("important note") have lower CTR. The structural rule: the warning must deliver a specific actionable insight, not generic doom.

## When to use
High-CTR slot for tactical / advisory content. Loses effectiveness when over-used (audiences pattern-match "WARNING" as boilerplate).
