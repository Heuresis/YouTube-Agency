# Title Specimen 023 — Universal-failure-pattern title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"Why Most [Audience] Never [Specific Outcome] (And What Actually Works)"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "Why Most [Professionals] Never Get Promoted (And What Actually Works)"
- "Why [Audience] Stay Stuck at [Stage] — and the Pattern That Breaks Through"
- "The Real Reason [Audience] Burn Out (It's Not What You Think)"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Universal-failure-pattern titles work because they trigger the viewer's identification with the stuck audience — the title implicitly asks "is this me?" The parenthetical "and what actually works" converts the title from depressing observation to actionable promise. The "it's not what you think" variant adds curiosity-gap density without overpromising.
