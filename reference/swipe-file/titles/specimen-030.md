# Title Specimen 030 — Start-over title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"If I Had to [Start Over / Restart / Rebuild] [Domain], Here's What I'd Do"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "If I Had to Start Over in [Domain] in [Current Year], Here's My Plan"
- "[Domain] in [Year]: What I'd Do Differently if I Started Today"
- "The [Domain] Plan I Wish I Had When I Started"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Start-over titles work because they leverage the creator's hindsight as proprietary knowledge — the audience values the lessons of someone further along the path. The "in [current year]" anchor adds urgency by signaling current-context relevance. The "I wish I had" variant adds vulnerability that earns trust.

- mode: authority

- channel stage: 100K–1M
