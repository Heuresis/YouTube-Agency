# Title Specimen 040 — Don't-do-until title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"Don't [Common Action] Until You Watch This"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "Don't [Common Decision] Until You Read This"
- "Stop Before You [Common Action] — This Will Save You"
- "Watch This Before You [Major Decision]"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Don't-do-until titles work because they convert the title into a friction-removal service — the audience views the click as protective rather than indulgent. The implicit promise is "I'll save you from a costly mistake." This title pattern is highest-CTR when paired with a thumbnail showing visible warning icons or a stop-gesture.
