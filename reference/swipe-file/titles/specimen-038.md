# Title Specimen 038 — Then-vs-now title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"[Domain] in [Year A] vs [Year B]: How [Specific Variable] Changed"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "[Domain] Then vs Now: What [Specific Variable] Looks Like"
- "[N Years Ago] vs Today: The [Specific Domain] Has Completely Changed"
- "Why [Domain] Is Different Now (and How To Adapt)"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Then-vs-now titles work because they leverage temporal contrast — the audience knows things have changed but wants to see the specific deltas. The "how to adapt" variant converts observation into actionable advice, which raises the value proposition. Specificity in years (e.g., 2015 vs 2025) outperforms generic "then" language.
