# Title Specimen 018 — How-I-X-in-Y title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"How I [Specific Outcome] in [Specific Timeframe] Without [Common Resource]"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "How I [Specific Revenue Outcome] in [N Months] Without [Conventional Strategy]"
- "How I [Closed N Clients] in [N Days] Using [Counterintuitive Method]"
- "How I [Built N-Person Team] Without [Conventional Resource]"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
How-I-X-in-Y titles convert specificity into curiosity — the specific number forces the brain to evaluate plausibility, and the "without" clause adds the counterintuitive angle that elevates the title from generic case-study. The combination of personal pronoun, specific outcome, specific timeframe, and constraint is the maximum density of credibility-anchors for a title.
