# Title Specimen 011 — Number-of-things title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"[N] [Things] That [Outcome / Common Property]"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "[15] Things I Learned After Making [$25M] For [Niche-X Clients]"
- "[7] Hidden [Strategies] [Top Operators] Use That You're Not"
- "[3] [Mistakes] Killing Your [Channel Growth] Right Now"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The number-of-things title gives the audience an explicit list-length expectation. Specific numbers (15, 7, 3) outperform round numbers (10, 5). The structural rule: numbers under 5 increase CTR but reduce watch-time; numbers over 10 reduce CTR but increase watch-time. 7–15 is the sweet spot for most educational content.

## When to use
Listicle / compilation videos. Less effective for narrative or single-thesis videos.
