# Title Specimen 031 — Hidden-phenomenon title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"The [Specific Phenomenon] No One Is Talking About"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "The [Domain] [Phenomenon] No One Wants To Discuss"
- "What's Really Happening in [Industry / Domain]"
- "The [Specific Trend] Nobody Sees Coming"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Hidden-phenomenon titles work because they tap into the audience's epistemic curiosity — what's true that I don't know? "No one is talking about" is an explicit claim of insider knowledge. The specificity in [Specific Phenomenon] is load-bearing; "the thing no one is talking about" doesn't trigger the response that "the [specific named pattern] no one is talking about" does.
