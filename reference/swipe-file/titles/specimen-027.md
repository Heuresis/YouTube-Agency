# Title Specimen 027 — Numbered-framework title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"The [N]-Step Framework I Use to [Specific Outcome]"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "The [N]-Step Framework I Use to [Outcome] (Stolen From [Generic Reference])"
- "My [N]-Part System For [Outcome] (After [N Years] of Iteration)"
- "The [Named Framework]: How I Approach [Specific Decision]"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Numbered-framework titles work because the number is a structural commitment — the audience knows what they're getting and how to evaluate it. The "I use" framing converts the title from theoretical to operator-tested. The "stolen from" variant adds humility that paradoxically increases credibility by acknowledging influence.
