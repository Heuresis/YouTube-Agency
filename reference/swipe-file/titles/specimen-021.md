# Title Specimen 021 — Transformation-claim title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"I [Specific Transformation Claim] in [Timeframe] (This Is What Worked)"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "I [Specific Body Composition Change] in [N Months] — This Is What Worked"
- "[N Pounds] in [Timeframe]: The Only Three Things That Mattered"
- "How I [Specific Physical Outcome] After [N Years] of Failing"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Transformation-claim titles work because the audience is intensely outcome-focused — they want to know if a result is achievable. The "this is what worked" parenthetical converts the title from a humble brag to a teaching frame. The "after N years of failing" variant adds vulnerability that earns trust without softening the outcome claim.
