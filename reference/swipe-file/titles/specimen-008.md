# Title Specimen 008 — Famous-figure-reframe title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"This Will Make [Famous Figure] [Specific Big Outcome]"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "This Will Make [Famous Creator] A Billionaire"
- "This Is Why [Famous Operator] Is About To 10x [Their Empire]"
- "[Famous Figure]'s Latest Move Will [Specific Big Outcome] (Here's Why Nobody's Talking About It)"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The famous-figure-reframe title borrows the famous figure's SEO / discovery gravity. The "nobody's talking about it" or "the move people are missing" angle is the load-bearing element — it converts the title from generic commentary into contrarian expert analysis.

## When to use
When the operator can credibly comment on a famous figure with tangentially-relevant credentials. Less effective if the operator has no standing to comment.
