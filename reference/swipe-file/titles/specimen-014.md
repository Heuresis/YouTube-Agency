# Title Specimen 014 — Behind-the-scenes title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"Behind the Scenes: The [Strategy] That Made Me [Specific Result]"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "Behind the Scenes: The [Funnel] That Made Me [$50K in 7 Days]"
- "Behind the Scenes: How I [Closed $1M in Sales] Last Quarter"
- "Inside My [Specific Project]: The Exact [System] That Generated [Specific Result]"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The behind-the-scenes title promises insider access. The "Behind the Scenes" framing converts the video from generic instruction into voyeuristic decomposition. The result must be specific.

## When to use
When the operator can show actual behind-the-scenes footage / data / artifacts. Without real backstage content, the title overpromises.
