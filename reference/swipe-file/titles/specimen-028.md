# Title Specimen 028 — Common-belief-is-wrong title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"[Common Belief] Is Wrong (Here's Why)"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "[Common Productivity Advice] Is Wrong (Here's The Data)"
- "Stop [Common Behavior]. Do This Instead."
- "The [Common Method] Trap (And What Actually Works)"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Common-belief-is-wrong titles work in saturated niches because the audience has heard every standard piece of advice and is primed for contrarian takes. The "here's the data" parenthetical converts the title from hot take to evidence-led claim. The "do this instead" variant front-loads action, which solution-aware audiences prefer.
