# Title Specimen 005 — Problem-solution direct title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"If You [Common Problem], Watch This"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "If You [Can't Get YouTube Views], Watch This"
- "If You [Struggle With Sales Calls], Watch This"
- "If You [Can't Get Past Your First $5K Month], Watch This"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The problem-solution title is the most directly-targeted variant — it explicitly addresses one specific problem and promises one specific viewing experience. The "Watch This" command is the load-bearing element; alternatives ("Try This") are softer and have lower CTR.

## When to use
Niche-targeted title slot when the operator has a specific solution to a specific problem. Less effective for broad-appeal content where the targeting is too narrow.
