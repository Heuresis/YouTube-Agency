# Title Specimen 024 — Small-audience-big-revenue title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"How I [Revenue Claim] With [Counterintuitively Small Audience]"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "How I Built A [Revenue Tier] Business With [Small Audience Size]"
- "[Revenue Claim] From [Tiny Audience]: The Math Most Creators Miss"
- "Why [Small Audience] Can Beat [Larger Audience] (Counterintuitive)"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Small-audience-big-revenue titles work because they invert the dominant volume-led growth narrative — creators are tired of being told they need more followers. The specific small-audience-size number is load-bearing; it triggers cognitive evaluation. The "math most creators miss" variant signals analytical depth and filters for serious viewers.

- mode: authority

- channel stage: 100K–1M
