# Title Specimen 007 — Peak-revenue-event title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"I Made [Specific Revenue] in [Specific Short Timeframe] With My [Vehicle], Here's Exactly How I Did It"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "I Made [$100K] in [30 Minutes] With My [Brand], Here's Exactly How I Did It"
- "I Made [$50K] in [a Single Week] With My [Service Business], Here's Exactly How I Did It"
- "I Made [$25K] in [One Day] With My [Course Launch], Here's Exactly How I Did It"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The peak-revenue-event title pairs an impossible-sounding revenue number with an impossibly-short timeframe. "Here's Exactly How I Did It" is the explicit promise of structural decomposition — it commits the body to a step-by-step walkthrough.

## When to use
When the operator has a specific peak-performance event. Vulnerable to "the timeframe is misleading" backlash if the body doesn't honor the title.
