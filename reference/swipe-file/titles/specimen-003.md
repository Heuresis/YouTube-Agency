# Title Specimen 003 — Contrarian angle formula

## Structure (paraphrased)
"Why [Common Belief] Is Wrong (And [Alternative])"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "Why [Posting Daily] Is Wrong (And [What Works Better])"
- "Why [Niching Down] Is Wrong (And [What 7-Figure Creators Actually Do])"
- "Why [Most Sales Advice] Is Wrong (And [Why Introverts Sell Better])"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The contrarian formula activates pattern interrupt. The directness in "Is Wrong" is the load-bearing element — softer phrasings have lower CTR. The alternative must be specific and credibly different.

## When to use
Saturated-niche pattern-interrupt slot. Less effective in unsaturated niches.
