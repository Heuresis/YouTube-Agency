# Title Specimen 022 — Deal-as-proof title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"How I [Closed / Bought / Acquired] [Property Type] for [Counterintuitive Resource]"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "How I Bought [Property Type] for [Tiny Down Payment / No Money Down]"
- "I Closed [N Deals] in [N Months] Without [Conventional Resource]"
- "The [N]-Door Strategy I Used to [Outcome] in [Market]"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Deal-as-proof titles work in real-estate because the audience can validate the claim — deals are concrete. The "counterintuitive resource" framing (no money down, with bad credit, in expensive market) is the structural hook; without that twist, the title reads as standard. Specific property type and outcome are load-bearing — generic "deal" doesn't trigger evaluation.
