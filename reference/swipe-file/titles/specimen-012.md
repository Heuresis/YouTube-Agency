# Title Specimen 012 — Trophy-moment title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"[Notable Figure] Gave Me / Us A [Specific Authority Artifact] — Here's How We Did It"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "[Industry Authority] Gave Us A [$1M/Mo Trophy] — Here's How We Did It"
- "[Tier-1 Operator] Featured Me on Their Show — Here's What Happened After"
- "I Won [Specific Industry Award] — Here's the Exact Path That Got Me There"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The trophy-moment title positions a tangible authority artifact as the proof object. The audience auto-asks "how did they earn this?" The body then reverse-engineers the path. The structural rule: the artifact must be specific and verifiable; vague "I've been recognized" doesn't work.

## When to use
When the operator has a specific authority artifact (award, public shoutout from a tier-1 figure, partnership announcement, milestone). Most effective when used at irregular intervals — over-using produces "all these are made up" backlash.
