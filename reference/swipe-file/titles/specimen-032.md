# Title Specimen 032 — Hidden-math title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"How [Audience] Actually [Specific Outcome] (The Math Nobody Shows You)"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "How [Wealthy Group] Actually [Build Wealth] (The Math Nobody Explains)"
- "The Real Math Behind [Common Aspiration]"
- "Why [Common Strategy] Doesn't Work (The Numbers Most Channels Hide)"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Hidden-math titles work in finance niches because the audience suspects they're being given simplified or misleading framings. The "math nobody shows you" framing promises numerical depth, which is the differentiator in the niche. The "actually" word is load-bearing; it signals the title will reveal what's true beneath the popular narrative.
