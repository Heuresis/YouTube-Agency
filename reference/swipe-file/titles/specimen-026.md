# Title Specimen 026 — Self-experiment title

## Structure (paraphrased)
"I [Tried / Did / Lived On] [Extreme Thing] for [Timeframe] — Here's What Happened"

## Worked structural examples (anonymized)
- "I Tried [Extreme Practice] for [Timeframe] — Here's What Happened"
- "[N Days] of [Specific Constraint] (The Results Surprised Me)"
- "I [Specific Experiment] for [Period] — This Is The Honest Outcome"

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
Self-experiment titles work because they signal first-person evidence — the creator put themselves on the line, which the audience respects. The "here's what happened" close is mandatory; without it, the title reads as pre-announcement rather than reflection. The "results surprised me" variant adds curiosity-gap without overpromising.
