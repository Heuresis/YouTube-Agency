# Hook Specimen 037 — Universal-pain productivity opener

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Beat 1 (0:00–0:05): Universal observation. "Most productivity advice doesn't work because it solves the wrong problem."
- Beat 2 (0:05–0:12): Identity-flag the tribe. "If you've tried [N popular productivity systems] and nothing has stuck, you're not the problem. The category is."
- Beat 3 (0:12–0:22): Reframe the problem. "The real bottleneck isn't time. It's [counterintuitive variable: e.g., decision-fatigue, identity-mismatch, environment design]."
- Beat 4 (0:22–0:32): Bound-promise. "I'll walk you through the [N]-part frame I use, the science behind it, and exactly how to set it up this weekend."

## Why this works (the imitation lesson — 2–4 sentences)
The universal-pain opener earns immediate alignment because most productivity-content viewers have already cycled through systems and feel like failures. Beat 2's "you're not the problem" frame is a powerful identity-relief move — it converts shame into curiosity. Beat 3's reframe to a counterintuitive variable is what differentiates the video from yet another tactic-stack, signaling depth.
