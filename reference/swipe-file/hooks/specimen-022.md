# Hook Specimen 022 — Multi-axis bound-promise hook

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Beat 1 (0–5s): Specific outcome + specific timeframe + specific multiplicative effect. "How I [achieved specific outcome] in [specific timeframe] without [common requirement]."
- Beat 2 (5–10s): Direct address to the qualified avatar. "If you're [specific avatar characteristic], this is the exact blueprint I wish I had when I was getting into things."
- Beat 3 (10–18s): Specific time commitment. "Lock in for the next [X] minutes because this is the most important [topic] video you'll watch this year."

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
This hook layers three credibility axes: time-bound outcome (Beat 1), avatar match (Beat 2), and time-commitment statement (Beat 3). The "wish I had" framing in Beat 2 positions the operator as the audience-from-the-future, which activates capability belief.

## When to use
Educational long-form videos where the operator wants to position the video itself as a discrete artifact ("blueprint," "playbook," "complete guide"). Less effective for episodic content.
