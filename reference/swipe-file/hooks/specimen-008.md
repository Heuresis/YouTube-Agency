# Hook Specimen 008 — Then-vs-Now contrast for reactive content

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Beat 1 (0–4s): Acknowledge the audience is using outdated playbooks. "Most people [are still doing the thing that worked in past-year], wondering why they can't get traction."
- Beat 2 (4–8s): Promise current-year insight. "But I'm about to show you EXACTLY what's working right now."
- Beat 3 (8–15s): Reinforce that the past-year approach is the failure mode. "Most people fail because they're trying to copy what worked in [past year]. They're following outdated playbooks."

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The then-vs-now hook activates urgency belief installation in the first 15 seconds. The audience auto-recognizes that they may be on the "then" side and must update — and the implicit promise of the rest of the video is the update path. Particularly effective when paired with a known platform/algorithm/tool change in the operator's niche.

## When to use
Reactive-content slot (the 10% Trend / Analysis pillar). Best timing is within 24–72 hours of a platform update / algorithm change / industry shift in the operator's niche. The shorter the time-to-publish, the higher the CTR spike.
