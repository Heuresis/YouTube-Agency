# Hook Specimen 016 — Compressed authority-stack hook

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Beat 1 (0–5s): Three layers of credentials in compressed form. "I [primary credential at named entity] for [N years]. My team [secondary credential — specific scale]. Now we run [current venture] which [specific result]."
- Beat 2 (5–10s): Pivot to value. "In this video, I'll show you the exact [framework / system] we use to [specific outcome]."

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The authority-stack hook is the "establish_authority" formula compressed to its minimum form. The structural rule: every credential must be specific — vague "I've been in the industry for years" doesn't work; specific "I was [role] at [named entity] for [N years]" does. The Beat 2 pivot to value must come within 10 seconds — longer authority introductions feel like a CV recital.

## When to use
Authority-mode videos where the operator has genuine multi-layer credentials. Less effective for operators whose credentials are thin or contestable; in those cases, lead with results (specimens 007, 012) instead.
