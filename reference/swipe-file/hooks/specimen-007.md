# Hook Specimen 007 — PAST + Social-proof hybrid with student transformations

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Beat 1 (0–3s): Specific personal time-and-financial transformation. "[N months/years] ago, I was [vulnerable starting state]."
- Beat 2 (3–8s): Specific present outcome. "Today, [high-status current state — quantified]."
- Beat 3 (8–15s): That same transformation extended to specific named students. "Same with my student [pseudonym/category] who [specific result]."
- Beat 4 (15–25s): Direct promise to walk through the exact mechanism.

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
The hybrid hook combines personal transformation (Beat 1–2) with multi-person social proof (Beat 3) within the first 15 seconds. This pre-installs both Belief 3 (capability) and Belief 8 (trust in expertise) before the body even begins. The structural rule: the student case-study in Beat 3 must be specific enough to feel real (named demographic, specific result, specific timeframe) — generic "students of mine make money" reads as fake.

## When to use
Conversion-tilted authority videos where the operator's offer is high-ticket coaching or education. Less suitable for pure-reach videos because the offer-adjacent framing reduces broad appeal.
