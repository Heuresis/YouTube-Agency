# Loop-Architecture Specimen 062 — Chapter-loop architecture

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Master loop opens in the cold-open at 0:00–0:08.
- Chapter 1 opens its own loop: a sub-question that won't fully resolve until later.
- Chapter 2 opens its own loop, references Chapter 1's lingering question.
- Chapter 3 closes Chapter 1's loop, opens its own.
- Chapter 4 closes Chapter 2's loop, deepens Chapter 3's loop.
- Final chapter closes Chapter 3's loop and the master loop simultaneously.
- Cross-chapter callbacks: each chapter explicitly references at least one prior chapter.

## Why this works (the imitation lesson — 2–4 sentences)
Chapter-loop architecture is the structurally most sophisticated retention technique known, used in long-form documentary content. The interlocked closures (chapter 3 closes chapter 1; chapter 4 closes chapter 2) are the load-bearing element; the audience cannot leave any chapter without sacrificing other chapters' resolutions. The technique requires extensive scripting; ad-libbed long-form rarely achieves it.
