# Hook Specimen 012 — Bound-promise + Social-proof opener

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Beat 1 (0–4s): Specific named timeframe + specific named result for a specific named-or-typed student. "[Student type] went from [low starting state] to [specific result] in [specific timeframe]."
- Beat 2 (4–10s): Pivot to the explicit promise of the video. "And in this video I'll show you exactly how they did it."
- Beat 3 (10–20s): Establish the operator's role. "I'm [name/role], I [credential], and [student] is one of [N] students I've helped do this."

## Why this works (the imitation lesson)
This hook front-loads the social proof + the time-bounded promise + the operator's authority in the first 20 seconds. The structural rule: Beat 1's specifics must match what the body of the video will actually deliver. If the hook claims "30 days" but the body reveals it took 90, trust collapses immediately.

## When to use
Authority-mode videos where the operator's offer is built around specific student outcomes. Best paired with on-camera artifacts (screenshots of student results, video testimonials) in the body.
