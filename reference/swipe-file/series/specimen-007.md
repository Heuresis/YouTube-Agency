# Series-Structure Specimen 007 — Topic deep-dive series

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Video 1 — The introduction: What is [topic], at high altitude. Why anyone should care.
- Video 2 — The history: How [topic] developed. Key inflection points.
- Video 3 — How it actually works: The technical / structural mechanism.
- Video 4 — The economics: Who profits, who loses, what the incentives are.
- Video 5 — The implications: What [topic] means for you, your industry, the future.
- Visual continuity: Shared title-card style, consistent illustrative style across videos.

## Why this works (the imitation lesson — 2–4 sentences)
Topic deep-dive series earn unaware-audience retention by structuring complex domains into digestible episodes — each video functions as a chapter in a larger book. The order (intro → history → mechanism → economics → implications) is structurally important; reversing it ("here are the implications first") loses viewers who don't yet care. Visual continuity is what signals series rather than disconnected videos.
