# Series-Structure Specimen 001 — 8-belief installation series

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Video 1 — Desirability of outcome: Why [outcome] is worth wanting in your specific context.
- Video 2 — Worthiness: Why someone like you is the kind of person who can have this.
- Video 3 — Capability: Why this is achievable, with evidence of others like you doing it.
- Video 4 — Timing: Why now is the right window, structurally.
- Video 5 — Mechanism: How the actual transformation works (the framework).
- Video 6 — Better-than-alternatives: Why this approach beats the obvious alternatives.
- Video 7 — Trust-in-person: Who you are, why your story is credible.
- Video 8 — Trust-in-expertise: The depth of your knowledge / credentials / track record.
- Pre-launch CTA (across all 8): Soft mention of the product, increasing intensity in videos 6–8.
- Launch (synchronized with Video 8 release): Full open of the offer.

## Why this works (the imitation lesson — 2–4 sentences)
The 8-belief installation series is the canonical structural pattern for high-ticket back-end conversion — each video installs one of the structural beliefs the prospect must hold to buy. Videos 1–4 build emotional / identity infrastructure; videos 5–8 build rational / evidence infrastructure. The order matters; reversing it (rational first, emotional second) reduces conversion because the rational layer can't land without the prior emotional installation.

## When this specimen is in the candidate pool
- mode: authority
- audience awareness: solution-aware to product-aware
- creator persona compatibility: structured / advisor / patient
- channel stage: 100K–1M
- back-end offer fit: high-ticket coaching, mastermind, cohort
- timing: 8–12 weeks pre-launch

## Production notes
- Length per video: 12–22 minutes
- Total series length: ~2.5 hours of video
- Production complexity: medium-high (consistent visual identity across series)
- Series interdependency: each video stands alone but compounds when watched in order
