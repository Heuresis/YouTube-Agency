# Series-Structure Specimen 012 — Identity-pivot series

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Video 1 — The current identity: Who you currently are as [audience]. What that identity makes hard. What it limits.
- Video 2 — The aspirational identity: Who [target identity] is. What they do, how they think, how they're built.
- Video 3 — The bridge: How identity actually shifts (not through tactics, through deliberate practice + new contexts).
- Video 4 — The first identity-shift practice: A concrete, accessible practice that begins the shift.
- Video 5 — The deeper identity-shift practices: For people who've started.
- Video 6 — The community of [target identity]: Where to find them, why proximity matters.
- Cross-video continuity: Each video reinforces the same target identity from a different angle.

## Why this works (the imitation lesson — 2–4 sentences)
Identity-pivot series are the highest-leverage authority asset for high-ticket back-end conversion because they install the identity that makes the offer feel necessary. Video 6's "proximity matters" framing is the load-bearing conversion-driver; it positions the back-end community / cohort as the answer to the identity-shift problem the series surfaced. The series must reinforce the same target identity across every video; identity-fragmentation across videos collapses the conversion math.
