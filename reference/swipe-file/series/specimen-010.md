# Series-Structure Specimen 010 — Stage-by-stage progression series

## Structure (paraphrased)
- Video 1 — [Stage 0 to Stage 1]: How to get from absolute beginner to first signal of progress.
- Video 2 — [Stage 1 to Stage 2]: How to get from first signal to first real outcome.
- Video 3 — [Stage 2 to Stage 3]: How to scale beyond initial outcome.
- Video N — [Stage N-1 to Stage N]: How to reach mastery / scale.
- Cross-video callbacks: Each video references the prior stage and previews the next.
- Self-assessment opener: Each video begins with a brief "you should be on this video if you're at [stage]" gate.

## Why this works (the imitation lesson — 2–4 sentences)
Stage-by-stage series earn long-term audience retention because viewers progress through the series as they progress in their journey — the channel becomes a structured curriculum. The self-assessment opener (Beat: self-assessment opener) is the load-bearing element; without it, viewers at the wrong stage waste time on inappropriate content and drop off. Each stage's content must be specific to that stage; generic advice across stages collapses the value.
