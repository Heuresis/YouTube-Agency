# Authority-Provocation Archetype

## Origin archetype
Comparative study of polarizing public-figure brand machines, decomposed into structural components by brand strategists and recast at reduced amplitude for creator channels.

## What it solves
A small number of public figures have compounded from zero to durable followings in 18–36 months by running the same eight-part structure. The structure itself is mechanical and extractable. What is *not* extractable safely is the amplitude: run at full volume, the same components produce guru drift — manufactured outrage, enemies lists, audience burnout, and the trust-contract violations the OS prohibits (INV-16). This framework documents the eight components **with the dial that keeps them operator-grade**: modest polarization, methodology-led, evidence-anchored.

## Structure — the eight components

| # | Component | What it actually is | Operator-dial example (educator channel) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Polarizing stance** | One non-negotiable position the creator defends for years | "Most channel advice optimizes for views. A creator with a real offer should optimize for qualified watch time — even when it costs reach." |
| 2 | **Public identity** | A compressed, recognizable self-description and approach | "The [niche] educator who ships receipts, not theory" — five to seven words the audience can repeat |
| 3 | **Out-group naming** | A credible *category of approach* the creator stands against — never named individuals | "View-count gurus," "engagement-bait growth channels," "course-sellers who've never run the system they teach" |
| 4 | **Broadcast cadence** | Ritual output rhythm past the recognition threshold | One long-form per week + 2–3 community posts, same days, same shape — not 15/day, not 1/month |
| 5 | **Product as outcome** | The back-end isn't "a program"; it's the delivery of a named operational outcome | "I don't sell a course. I sell the working [named system] installed on your channel/business" |
| 6 | **Anti-incumbent frame** | Structural critique of the dominant industry approach — method, not person | "The sub-count obsession is structurally wrong for educator channels; here's the arithmetic" |
| 7 | **Iteration as proof** | Public iteration, failed experiments, honest rebuilds | "This video flopped — the retention post-mortem, and what v2 changes" |
| 8 | **Signature visuals** | 2–3 recurring aesthetic motifs recognizable at glance speed | One set/backdrop, one thumbnail grammar, one typography — defended for a year |

The archetype works when **at least six of eight** are active simultaneously. Below five, the channel reads as just-another-educator — no gravity. Two or three components run in isolation are worse than one done well; half-built, the archetype is a collection of suspicious-looking parts.

## Structure — the operator dial vs the full dial

The full-dial version is what the maximal public figures run: existential stakes, all-platform saturation, conflict worn as costume. It produces enormous ceilings *and* the documented collapse pattern (see `parasocial-ethical-guardrails`). The operator dial keeps every component and reduces the amplitude:

| Component | Full dial (maximal public figure) | Operator dial (creator with a real back-end) |
|---|---|---|
| Polarizing stance | Existential, cultural ("the modern world is broken") | Market-category contrarian ("the standard advice in this niche is structurally wrong for X") |
| Public identity | Lifestyle/persona theater (uniform, props, spectacle) | Methodology + reputation ("the person who fixes [named problem]") |
| Out-group naming | Systemic enemies ("the establishment") | Category-specific approaches, never people |
| Broadcast cadence | 10–20 pieces/day across every platform | Sustainable single-platform rhythm; YouTube-first, expanded later |
| Product as outcome | Membership in a worldview | A specific, measurable operational outcome |
| Anti-incumbent | Anti-institutional everything | Anti-incumbent-*method*, with evidence |
| Iteration as proof | Public conflicts and bans worn as martyrdom | Public post-mortems and honest rebuilds |
| Signature visuals | Intimidating / movement-style conformity | Simple, repeatable, recognizable |

The operator dial sacrifices the mega-following ceiling. It buys durable niche reputation that compounds over 3–5 years — which is the actual revenue surface of a creator selling a high-ticket back-end to a qualified audience, not a mass-membership audience.

## Why the operator dial compounds
1. **Polarizing stance creates filter strength.** Fence-sitters defect; fit viewers deepen — and fit viewers refer at far higher rates than a neutral audience.
2. **Out-group naming creates referral scaffolding.** "If you're sick of [category's approach], watch this channel" is a sentence an audience member can actually say (see `four-community-markers`, Marker 2).
3. **Broadcast cadence installs sacred vocabulary.** Phrases repeated past the embarrassment threshold enter the niche's speech (Marker 1).
4. **Product-as-outcome moves the buying decision from feature comparison to outcome acquisition** — which is where premium pricing lives.
5. **Iteration-as-proof converts would-be brand damage into credibility.** A public "we got this wrong, here's the rebuild" reads as operator-grade honesty — and it is the antidote to the curated-perfection pattern audiences have learned to distrust.
6. **Signature visuals collapse recognition cost.** Sub-second recognition in a crowded feed is a distribution moat.
7. **Anti-incumbent framing inherits pre-existing audience energy.** The niche already resents the approaches that failed them; the frame channels that energy at zero added cost.

## The 90-day build (sequenced after proof exists)
The archetype anchors on receipts. **Do not run this build before the channel has demonstrable outcomes** — its own or its students'; an archetype with nothing underneath is costume.

- **Weeks 1–2 — Define.** Write the polarizing stance in one sentence (if it makes no incumbent uncomfortable, it's too safe). Name the out-group as a category of approach. Compress the public identity to 5–7 words.
- **Weeks 3–4 — Stock.** Choose the 2–3 signature visuals and defend them. Coin 3–5 sacred phrases that will appear on every surface.
- **Weeks 5–8 — Broadcast.** One platform, fixed rhythm. Every piece touches at least two of the eight components. ~30 published pieces is where recognition starts registering.
- **Weeks 9–12 — Reinforce.** Engage the named category-of-approach publicly, politely, structurally. Begin routing to the back-end using the vocabulary now live in the audience's comments.

Day-90 measure: can warm-audience members name the stance, the out-group, the sacred phrases, and the offer's outcome? Under ~20% recognition → run another 90 days before scaling the offer push.

## What this archetype is NOT
- **Not volume alone.** Cadence without stance is noise.
- **Not controversy for its own sake.** Random heat burns the niche network; the stance must be defensible with evidence and held for years.
- **Not a persona separate from the creator.** Audiences detect a persona-creator gap within 60–90 days; the archetype must be a *compression* of the real person (this is the parasocial-trust contract — INV-16).
- **Not ethical by default.** Every component has a dark mirror (below). The archetype is a scalpel; it cuts whatever it's pointed at.
- **Not permanent without maintenance.** Recognition decays in 90–180 days without ongoing broadcast and stance-defense.
- **Not compatible with flicker.** Components can be dialed; they cannot alternate. Contrarian Monday / hedged Wednesday reads as unstable.

## The dark-mirror table (guru-drift detection)

| Component | Healthy operator-dial expression | Pathological full-dial expression |
|---|---|---|
| Polarizing stance | Contrarian, evidence-reasoned | Contrarian for attention alone |
| Public identity | Authentic compression of the real creator | A costume the creator can't sustain |
| Out-group naming | Category of approach | Personal enemies list |
| Broadcast cadence | Consistent rhythm + value | Volume masking emptiness |
| Product as outcome | Real outcome, delivered and documented | Identity theater with no delivery receipts |
| Anti-incumbent | Method critique with proof | Cynical delegitimization of all alternatives |
| Iteration as proof | Honest public correction | Manufactured martyrdom ("they silenced me") |
| Signature visuals | Recognizable aesthetic | Conformity pressure on the audience |

The line between columns is usually one decision made under revenue pressure. The standing restraint protocol and quarterly review live in `parasocial-ethical-guardrails` — read that file alongside this one, never this one alone.

## When to use
At positioning time (`extract-creator-voice`, `build-channel-architecture`), when calibrating the channel's public-voice amplitude (`content-mix-strategy`), and at every quarterly review — both to check component coverage (≥6 of 8 active) and to run the dark-mirror scan.

## Why it works
Attention compounds around figures who are *predictable in stance and surprising in content*. The eight components manufacture exactly that: the stance, identity, out-group, and visuals make the channel predictable (recognizable, referable, quotable); the cadence and iteration keep it surprising. Neutral channels are forgettable by construction — there is nothing to repeat to a friend.

## Anti-patterns
- Running the build before proof exists (archetype-as-costume).
- Escalating amplitude when growth stalls — the drift sequence documented in `parasocial-ethical-guardrails` begins exactly here.
- Borrowing another creator's stance — an unowned stance can't be defended under pressure, and the audience watches the defense, not the claim.
- Polarizing on worldview instead of methodology — worldview polarization imports audiences the back-end can't serve and repels qualified buyers (INV-8 scope discipline applies to positioning too).

## Variants observed
Some operators teach a softened five-component version (dropping out-group, anti-incumbent, and provocation entirely). It is safer and measurably weaker: without contrast components the archetype loses its referral scaffolding. Canonical form retained: all eight, with the dial — amplitude is the safety mechanism, not amputation.
