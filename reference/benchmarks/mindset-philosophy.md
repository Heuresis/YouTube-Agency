# Mindset & Philosophy — Niche Benchmarks

> Typical structural performance bands observed in mindset / philosophy / self-improvement / spirituality YouTube channels. Bands are calibrated per-channel from real data over time.

## Niche scope

Mindset / philosophy includes: applied philosophy, mindset coaching, self-improvement, identity transformation, founder psychology, contemplative practice, depth psychology, narrative psychology, Stoicism / existentialism / Buddhist-applied content. Audience tilt: high parasocial trust, slow audience commitment, deep retention once committed, identity-aspirational positioning.

## CTR ranges by length-band

Long-form (10–35 min):
- Floor band: 4–5%
- Typical band: 5–8%
- Upper band: 8–12%
- Outlier: 12%+

CTR is moderate for this niche. The hooks are quieter (open-question, identity-aspirational), so high-CTR-equivalent content typically requires either a contrarian frame layered on top OR a narrative-stake hook strong enough to compete with louder niches.

Short-long (4–8 min):
- Floor: 5–6%
- Typical: 6–10%
- Upper: 10–13%

Shorts:
- Typical: 6–11%
- Upper: 12–18%

## AVD ranges by length-band

15-minute videos:
- Floor APV: 45%
- Typical APV: 50–60%
- Upper APV: 62%+

25-minute videos:
- Floor APV: 40%
- Typical APV: 45–55%
- Upper APV: 58%+

35-minute videos:
- Floor APV: 35%
- Typical APV: 40–48%
- Upper APV: 50%+

Mindset / philosophy AVD bands are unusually high relative to other niches. The audience commits deeply once they commit; the slow-pace philosophical format earns long-form retention.

## RPM bands

Mindset / philosophy is mid-CPM. Typical bands:

- Floor: $6
- Typical: $10–$18
- Upper: $18–$28
- Outlier: $28+ — typically when audience composition skews high-income (founder-philosophy / executive-coaching-adjacent)

Sub-niche variation:
- General mindset / self-improvement: $8–$15 RPM
- Founder psychology / executive mindset: $15–$28 RPM
- Spirituality / contemplative: $5–$12 RPM
- Identity transformation / coaching-adjacent: $10–$20 RPM

## Sponsor CPM bands

Sponsor CPM in mindset / philosophy:

- Floor: $20 per CPM
- Typical: $30–$60 per CPM
- Upper: $60–$100 per CPM

Common sponsor categories: meditation / habit apps, coaching platforms, mindfulness tools, journaling tools, mental-health adjacent (with regulatory care), book / audiobook platforms.

## Retention curve archetypes

- **The narrative climb** — common for story-driven philosophical content. Drops in first half, recovers through resolution.
- **The slow-burn flat** — typical of mindset / philosophical creator archetype. Slow but stable retention.
- **The identity-aspirational close** — strong end-retention as the close lands; identity-transformation framing pulls viewers to the final minute.

## Recurring topic categories that perform

- **"What [philosopher / tradition] teaches about [modern problem]"** — applied philosophy; high evergreen demand.
- **"Why [common belief] is wrong about [identity / mindset]"** — contrarian frame in this niche.
- **"The [psychological] pattern that's keeping you stuck"** — pattern-naming; mechanism-first overlap.
- **"What I learned from [years] of [practice]"** — first-person reflection; story-driven.
- **"The question I had to sit with for [time]"** — open-question hook; signature pattern.
- **"How to [become / develop / overcome]"** — transformation content.

## Audience composition characteristics

- **Age**: 25–55 typical primary; 18–25 secondary
- **Gender**: variable by sub-niche; founder-philosophy skews male; contemplative-spirituality more balanced
- **Geography**: US dominant; UK / Australia / Western-Europe secondary; emerging engagement in non-Anglophone Western Europe
- **Income**: variable; founder-mindset audiences are typically higher-income
- **Education**: typically college-educated; humanities-leaning sub-niches

## Conversion benchmarks

For channels with a back-end offer in mindset / philosophy:

- **View-to-application** (for high-ticket coaching / mastermind): 0.5–2% on pinned VSL
- **Application-to-call**: 30–50% (audiences are more thoughtful; lower application rate is normal)
- **Call show rate**: 65–75%
- **Call-to-close rate**: 20–35% on coaching offers ($3K–$10K typical price point)

LTV per email subscriber: typically $80–$300 calibrated; recalibrate per-channel.

## Regulatory considerations

Lower regulatory burden than finance / health, but:

- **Mental-health adjacent claims**: avoid clinical-mental-health claims unless licensed; refer-to-professional disclaimer where appropriate
- **Earnings / lifestyle claims**: substantiated only
- **Spiritual / religious sensitivity**: respectful; non-prescriptive; the parasocial-trust contract in this niche is fragile to perceived dogmatism

## Banned content patterns

- Manufactured emotional manipulation (fake-stakes hook, manufactured outrage)
- Engagement-bait disguised as philosophical question (INV-17)
- Claims about mental-health outcomes without licensing
- Inauthentic spiritual / religious framing (the niche detects this immediately)

## Calibration note

Mindset / philosophy is a slow-burn niche. New channels typically take 6–18 months to find audience-fit; bands recalibrate as the audience composition stabilizes. The patient-channel benefit: AVD bands stay high relative to other niches once the audience finds the channel, and back-end-offer LTV is durable.
